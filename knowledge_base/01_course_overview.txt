COURSE OVERVIEW — B.Tech Computer Science and Computer Engineering (CSCE)
KIIT University, Bhubaneswar, Odisha
========================================================================

PROGRAM: Bachelor of Technology in Computer Science and Computer Engineering (CSCE)
DURATION: 4 Years (8 Semesters)
CURRENT BATCH: 2023–2027
SCHOOL: School of Computer Engineering, KIIT University

ACCREDITATION & RECOGNITION:
- NAAC A++ accredited institution
- NBA accredited programs
- Ranked among top 10 private universities in India (NIRF 2024)
- UGC recognized, AICTE approved

TOTAL CREDITS FOR GRADUATION: 160
  - Core Theory Credits: 90
  - Core Lab Credits: 24
  - Elective Credits: 24 (Open: 12, Professional: 12)
  - Project / Internship Credits: 22

- Engineering Physics — 3 credits
- Programming in C — 3 credits
- Engineering Drawing & Graphics — 2 credits

- Physics Lab, C Programming Lab — 2 credits each

- Java Lab, Electronics Lab — 2 credits each

Semester 3:
- Data Structures & Algorithms — 4 credits
- Discrete Mathematics — 3 credits
- Computer Organization & Architecture — 3 credits
- Operating Systems — 3 credits
- Database Management Systems — 3 credits
- DS Lab, OS Lab — 2 credits each

Semester 4:

Semester 5:

PROGRAM EDUCATIONAL OBJECTIVES (PEOs):
PEO-1: Graduates will apply technical knowledge to solve real-world computational problems.
PEO-2: Graduates will design and develop software systems meeting industry standards.
PEO-3: Graduates will demonstrate professional ethics, teamwork, and leadership.
PEO-4: Graduates will engage in lifelong learning and adapt to emerging technologies.

ATTENDANCE POLICY:
- Minimum 75% attendance is mandatory in every subject.
- Students below 75% may be debarred from end-semester exams.
- Medical condonation allowed up to 10% with supporting documents.
- Attendance is tracked through biometric and manual registers.

TEACHING METHODOLOGY:
- Theory: 3–4 lectures per week per subject
- Labs: 2–3 hours per week per lab
- Industry experts as visiting faculty
- Project-based learning from Semester 5 onward
- International student exchange programs available

FACILITIES:
- Central Library: IEEE, Springer, Elsevier, ACM Digital Library access
- GPU Computing Lab for AI/ML courses
- High-speed Wi-Fi (1 Gbps backbone) across campus
- KIIT Technology Business Incubator (KIIT-TBI)
- 25+ international partner universities for exchange
